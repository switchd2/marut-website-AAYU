THE TECHNOLOGY

One FCU. Three modes. Infinite possibilities.

FIXED WING

Optimised PID loops for fixed-wing aerodynamics, with full support for traditional rudder/elevator/aileron configurations.

VTOL

Seamless transition logic between hover and cruise modes. Handles tilt-rotor, tailsitter, and hybrid VTOL configurations.

MULTIROTOR

Battle-tested multirotor firmware with support for quadcopters, hexacopters, octocopters, and custom frame geometries.

All three modes. One unified codebase. One community.
